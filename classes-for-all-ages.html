RYUGEN TAIKO PERFORMING GROUP


We welcome taiko players with at least 5 years of kumi daiko (group taiko) experience who do not currently belong to any taiko group. We are looking to start a performance taiko trainee group to eventually perform with us in events. Please inquire with us if you are interested by either emailing us or simply give us a call. We look forward to hearing from you!

YAGURA-GUMI

​

Currently we are looking for more Yagura-gumi (bon dance taiko or bon taiko) participants that would like to join us. No experience necessary! Currently we are accepting kids from 6 years old all the way to senior citizen ages! Classes are FREE! You can email us or call us for more information.
